About

Strategic Counsel for Leaders

IBPROM Corp is a federally incorporated Canadian advisory firm founded by Joseph Soares, a seasoned executive with deep expertise in strategic communications, leadership development, and reputation intelligence.

We work with C-suite executives, founders, and public-facing professionals to protect, build, and leverage their reputations as strategic business assets. Our methodology combines real-time digital intelligence with proven crisis response frameworks and proactive brand positioning.
